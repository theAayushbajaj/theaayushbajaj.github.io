## Whats the catch?

What I can think of is to gather data around social media and news and ofcourse a source that found recently the GDELT project which is a home of tons of data. so by thorough analysis and context mapping for different candidates/parties I will be able to see the wave of people's sentiment towards a campaign or a thing like caa protest, whether people still have preference for bjp then what are those reasons for it,from what demographic they are from. A complimentary website could help showcase all these with few options to generate sort of a approval rating like that in the west but that is only from public opninion but we know how hypocrite poeple are.
